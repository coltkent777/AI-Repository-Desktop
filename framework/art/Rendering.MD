# Purpose

This document defines the permanent rendering standards for artwork created using the Art Framework.

These standards establish the baseline visual identity for every campaign. Individual campaigns may document approved stylistic exceptions, but they do not modify the framework itself.

---

# Rendering Standards

## Realism

Artwork should target approximately **7/10 realism**.

Images should feel believable and immersive while remaining clearly artistic. The objective is to capture the feeling of a master fantasy illustration rather than a photograph.

Photorealism is not the goal.

---

## Artistic Medium

The default rendering style is a hybrid of **fantasy concept art** and **high-quality illustration**.

Images should appear thoughtfully painted and intentionally composed, avoiding the appearance of computer-generated artwork.

---

## World Condition

The world should feel **lived in**.

Objects, clothing, armor, weapons, architecture, and environments should display believable craftsmanship, history, and use appropriate to the subject.

Imperfections should contribute to storytelling rather than clutter.

---

## Detail Density

Artwork should reward close inspection.

Fine details should reinforce the narrative, character, or setting.

Decorative elements should never exist solely to fill empty space.

Every visual detail should have purpose.

---

## Color

Rich, saturated color is encouraged.

Color should strengthen mood, storytelling, and visual hierarchy while maintaining harmony throughout the image.

Muted palettes should only be used when they intentionally support the scene.

---

## Lighting

Lighting should prioritize **readability before realism**.

Fantasy lighting is encouraged when it improves atmosphere or storytelling.

Even scenes described as dark should remain visually clear and enjoyable to view on a typical digital display.

The primary subject should always remain immediately recognizable.

---

## Magic

Magic should always feel like a natural part of the world.

Its visual presentation may range from:

- Subtle and mundane
- Calm and ancient
- Ethereal and mysterious
- Cinematic and spectacular

The presentation should always support the narrative rather than exist solely for visual spectacle.

---

## Quality Standard

Generated artwork should minimize recognizable AI artifacts.

Images should be rejected if they contain distracting defects, including but not limited to:

- Plastic or artificial-looking skin
- Anatomical errors
- Duplicate or merged objects
- Incoherent ornamentation
- Unintentional visual clutter
- Rendering inconsistencies
- Any artifact that distracts from the artwork

The final image should appear intentionally crafted rather than algorithmically assembled.

---

# Campaign Exceptions

Campaigns may intentionally deviate from these standards.

Any deviation must be explicitly documented within that campaign's `STYLE.md`.

Campaign exceptions supplement the framework.

They do not modify the framework itself.
