# Children of the Nat One

> **Campaign Status:** Approved

## Campaign Identity

### Campaign Name

Children of the Nat One

### Campaign Description

A group of adventurers works to stop a curse spreading across the land
while constantly being pulled into family hijinks and unexpected
adventures. Every adventure begins in **Candlekeep**, Faerûn's legendary
library, with each story unfolding from the discovery of a book.

### Core Themes

-   Exploration
-   Self-discovery
-   Found family
-   Becoming more attuned to the world
-   Protecting and saving others

### Overall Tone

The campaign blends serious storytelling with heavy comedy, family
hijinks, adventure, danger, and moments of genuine horror. Its tone
evolves naturally with the party, creating a well-rounded experience
that balances humor and high stakes.

### Visual Identity

The visual heart of the campaign is **Candlekeep**, a library the size
of a castle that serves as the beginning of every adventure. The world
is typically bright and full of life until events suddenly take a darker
turn. The party is unusually large and diverse, composed of many races,
classes, sizes, and appearances, making them impossible to ignore
wherever they travel.
