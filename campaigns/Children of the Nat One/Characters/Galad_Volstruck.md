# Narrative Information

## Character Summary

Adopted from an orphanage into the prestigious House Volstruck, Galad
was raised among battle mages, assassins, and masters of the mystic
arts. Rather than specialize immediately, he pursued languages,
scholarship, and arcane mastery with the goal of becoming equally
capable in refined magical theory and lethal precision. Freshly
graduated from his academy, he now begins forging his own legacy.

## Personality Traits

-   Nothing can shake his optimistic attitude.
-   Enjoys fine food, quality drink, and high society.
-   Rough living grates on him.
-   Comfortable killing when necessary, but never wasteful.

## Ideal

**Power (Lawful)** --- He intends to rise to the highest ranks of
influence within his faith.

## Organizations

-   House Volstruck (Nobility)
-   Candlekeep --- Seeker Status

## Allies

-   Members of House Volstruck by blood or patronage.

## Enemies / Dislikes

-   Anyone who abuses or exploits orphans.

# Physical Appearance

Galad is classically handsome with refined infernal features. He
possesses a lean, athletic build rather than overwhelming musculature.

His long black hair is meticulously maintained. Elegant horns sweep
backward with his hair, subtly textured and polished to a near
mirror-like sheen. He carries a traditional devil's tail.

His posture is proud, his stride deliberate, and he maintains unwavering
eye contact. He smiles rarely, favoring a composed scowl that reinforces
his quiet confidence.

## First Impression

Galad immediately projects intelligence, refinement, and dangerous
competence. His presence naturally inspires fascination or caution.

## Signature Motif

# Continuity Notes

Future artwork should preserve Galad's refined noble appearance,
disciplined magical style, and understated but intimidating presence. He
should feel elegant, intelligent, and quietly dangerous rather than
overtly monstrous.
