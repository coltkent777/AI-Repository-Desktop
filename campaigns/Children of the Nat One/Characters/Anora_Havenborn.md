-   Race: Half-Elf
-   Class: Bard
-   Subclass: College of Dance ("College of Song" within the campaign)

-   Background: Noble

-   Age: 21
-   Size: Medium
-   Height: 5'2"

-   Hair: Long chocolate brown fading into silver tips
-   Eyes: Large, bright, expressive lavender
-   Skin: Fair with a gentle rose flush
-   Faith: None

## Narrative

Anora Havenborn was raised in Havenmere Glade by her overprotective
parents, who convinced her she possessed immense magical power. Leaving
home shattered that illusion, but she gradually discovers that her true
gift has always been her extraordinary voice, compassion, and charisma.

## Personality

-   Radiantly charismatic
-   Hopelessly inexperienced
-   Confident beyond her experience
-   Lovingly spoiled
-   Deeply compassionate
-   Naturally optimistic
-   Theatrically dramatic
-   Fierce when protecting those she loves

## Organizations

-   Candlekeep --- Seeker Status

## Physical Appearance

A petite half-elf with long chocolate-brown hair fading into silver
tips, expressive lavender eyes, and an infectious smile. She radiates
warmth, optimism, and effortless magnetism.

## Equipment

**Arcane Focus:** Her voice.

Although she carries a pink quarterstaff she affectionately calls her
"magic wand," it is symbolic rather than functional.

**Signature Equipment** - Pink wand-like quarterstaff - Animated Bloom

## Arcane Identity

**Magical Presence:** Uplifting, comforting, emotionally resonant.

**Signature Casting Style:** A heartfelt performance powered by speech,
music, and emotion.

**Signature Colors** - Soft Gold - Rose Gold - Bright Pink

**Favorite Spell:** Vicious Mockery

## Visual Design Language

**Keywords** - Sweet - Fierce - Charismatic

**First Impression**

A bright, bubbly, innocent young half-elf whose warmth immediately draws
others toward her. She possesses a magnetic presence that people
instinctively trust.

## Additional Notes

-   Can hold her breath for approximately seven minutes.
-   Within the campaign, the College of Dance is known as the College of
    Song.
-   Her voice---not her staff---is the defining symbol of her magic.
