# Narrative Information

## Character Summary

Ithrael Sunwell is a sworn Knight of the Order of the Sunbound Paladins, devoted to protecting the innocent and preserving the sanctity of life. The loss of his celestial wife transformed grief into purpose, inspiring him to become the shield he wished had existed when tragedy struck. Every sunrise renews his vow to defend the helpless and oppose the darkness.

## Personality Traits

- Speaks and acts with unwavering calm.
- Brings order to chaos through quiet confidence.
- Honors both the living and the dead.
- Leads through example.

# Continuity Notes

Ithrael should always be portrayed as a beacon of hope. His immaculate armor, sunrise lighting, and white, gold, and cerulean palette should reinforce his role as the party's steadfast guardian. The Dawnring worn beneath his armor remains a constant visual reminder of the promise that drives him.
