# Narrative Information

## Character Summary

Liriel Amaranthae Vaelwyn was born beneath a rare celestial conjunction in the Western Heartlands to House Vaelwyn, a wealthy family whose prosperity was built upon generations of honoring ancient druidic pacts rather than exploiting the land they stewarded. From an early age she heard whispers carried upon the wind from a celestial ancestor whose spirit remained forever intertwined with the natural order.

Rejecting the comfortable political future expected of her, Liriel embraced both her noble heritage and her calling as a druid. Today she uses the wealth, influence, and mercantile reach of House Vaelwyn to quietly undermine tyrants, slavers, and those who exploit the natural world while funding druid circles and protecting sacred places throughout Faerûn. Whether walking among nobles or beneath moonlit forests, she answers only to the balance of nature.

## Personality Traits

- Fiercely protective of the innocent.
- Deeply devoted to preserving the natural world.
- Enjoys luxury when it is earned without cruelty or exploitation.
- Warm and compassionate beneath a composed exterior.

## Ideals

**Balance through Motion.** Stagnation is just as dangerous as destruction.

# Physical Appearance

Liriel is breathtakingly beautiful, possessing radiant celestial features softened by the quiet grace of an ancient druid. Her luminous skin seems almost kissed by moonlight, while vivid purple hair frames expressive violet eyes that seem to shimmer with hidden starlight.

When her celestial heritage manifests, magnificent violet wings unfurl behind her in a cascade of radiant feathers, creating an image that is both awe-inspiring and deeply serene.

# Divine & Nature Identity

# Additional Notes

- Can hold her breath for approximately twelve minutes.
- Frequently serves as a patron to druid circles across Faerûn.
- Believes civilization and nature can coexist only when neither seeks to dominate the other.
- Comfortable navigating both noble courts and untamed wilderness.
- Uses wealth as another tool to preserve balance rather than acquire power.

# Continuity Notes

Liriel should always feel like the union of celestial grace and ancient druidic wisdom. Her artwork should emphasize luminous moonlight, subtle celestial imagery, and elegant natural motifs rather than overwhelming magical spectacle. The Staff of Moonlight, violet wings, lunar symbolism, and restrained lavender-and-amethyst palette should remain consistent throughout future artwork. Even in moments of immense power, her magic should feel composed, beautiful, and timeless.
